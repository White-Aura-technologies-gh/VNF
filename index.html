The Visionary Norman Foundation is dedicated to creating sustainable change in communities by investing in the potential of young people. Through our comprehensive programs, we provide resources, opportunities, and support systems that empower youth to become leaders and change-makers.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through education, health, leadership, and philanthropy. We are guided by principles of empowerment, collaboration, innovation, equity, and inclusion to build a society where all people have equal access to essential needs, quality education, reliable healthcare, and meaningful opportunities for growth.

Our Vision

We envision a world rooted in equity, dignity, and sustainable development where transformative change arises when communities are equipped with resources, knowledge, and collective spirit to overcome adversity and chart their own course toward prosperity and well-being.

Our Leadership

Abraham Norman Nortey

Founder & CEO

A recognized Ghanaian public health advocate, medical student, researcher, and distinguished leader in youth empowerment and community development. Recipient of the Kofi Annan Youth Inspirational Award and former President of the UCC Students Representative Council with multiple publications on public health topics.

Dr. Abena Yeboah

Director

Ayawaso East Municipal Health Director who brings extensive public health expertise and outreach design to the foundation. She plays a crucial role in developing and implementing health initiatives that serve communities across Ghana.

Dr. Sussie Anku

Medical Doctor & Reproductive Health Counselor

Leads adolescent health initiatives with a focus on reproductive health education and services. She has been instrumental in developing programs that address teenage pregnancy and provide comprehensive sexuality education in underserved communities.
